Mysuru City

Mysuru, also known as Mysore, is a city in the southern state of Karnataka in India. It is known for:

  • The Magnificent Mysuru Palace
  • The Beautiful Brindavan Gardens
  • The Chamundi Hills
  • The Mysuru Zoo
  • The Dasara Festival

Mysuru is also famous for its silk sarees, sandalwood carvings, and delicious Mysore Pak sweet.
